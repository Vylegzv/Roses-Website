Heirloom Roses

Tweet Follow @111iamtest

Heirloom Roses are specially bred at the Heirloom nursery. The goal of the nursery is to breed beautiful, fragrant roses in a wide range of colors in both the style of the ‘English’ roses and also to expand the range and quality of the shrub roses.


Apricot Impressionist

This rose was bred at the Heirloom nursery. It serves as an addition to the nursery's "Impressionist" line of fragrant climbing roses. It is a vigorous climbing rose that may be grown as a large shrub or climber. It produces rich, deep, apricot bronze buds that unfurl into many petaled blooms of a beautiful shade of apricot. This continual blooming climbing rose has all the charm and grace of an old garden rose.




Dancing In The Wind

This rose was bred at the Heirloom nursery. It serves as an outstanding addition to the Hybrid Musk class. It is a continually blooming, upright, and vigorous bush with glossy foliage. It produces huge clusters of soft, salmon pink, semi-double blooms, 12 to 15 petals each, with peachy-cream centers set off by rich golden stamens. This rose with a great charm and elegance is a real standout in the garden.
